Welcome to FeedMe

FeedMe helps you decide where to eat by showing one restaurant at a time based on your preferences. No more endless scrolling—just one solid pick at a time.

Privacy Policy

Data Collection: FeedMe does not collect personal data unless explicitly required for user-enabled features, such as saving preferences. We only request access to location and other necessary inputs if you enable filters or other optional features.

Third-Party Services: Restaurant information is pulled from third-party providers such as Google Places and Uber Eats. These platforms may have their own data policies which apply when you interact with their embedded features or links.

Tracking and Cookies: FeedMe does not use cookies or tracking tools to monitor your behavior across the web. We do not support targeted advertising or behavioral tracking of any kind.

Data Storage: If you opt to save preferences, they are stored securely and only associated with your device. No data is shared or sold to third parties.

User Rights: You may request deletion of saved preferences at any time by contacting us. We will delete all locally stored data associated with your use of the app.

Policy Changes: We may update this privacy policy to reflect improvements or changes to the app. If we make significant changes, we will notify users through an update or message within the app.

FAQ - How It Works

How does FeedMe choose a restaurant?
Based on your filters—like location, cuisine, rating, and price—we randomly show one solid choice at a time.

What if I don’t like the recommendation?
You can skip it, add it to a queue, or remove it from future suggestions.

Where does the data come from?
We use Google Places for restaurant data and integrate with services like Uber Eats for delivery options.

Is my data saved?
Not unless you opt-in to save preferences. We value privacy and don’t store personal info unless necessary.
